The Search Continues

Concerning the search for ever smaller sub-atomic particles, Werner Heisenberg has observed,

By getting to smaller and smaller units, we do not come to fundamental units, or indivisible units, but we do come to a point where division has no meaning.

Moreover, reductionism fails to help us explain real-world events. As Paul Davies, one of England's leading theoreticians, has noted,

It would be ludicrous to try to understand consciousness, or a living cell, or even an inanimate system such as a tornado, in terms of quarks, for example.
