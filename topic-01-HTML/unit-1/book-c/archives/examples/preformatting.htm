Assembling jumps & calls

This routine automatically assembles a short, near or far jump or call, depending on byte displacement, to the destination address. You can override such a jump or call by using a near or far prefix, as the following example shows: 

0100:0500  jmp   502            ; a 2-byte short jump

0100:0502  jmp   near  505      ; a 3-byte near  jump

0100:0505  jmp   far   50a      ; a 5-byte far   jump

You can abbreviate the near prefix to ne.
